💜 Our Story

Founded in 2018, Beauty Key began with a belief: everyone deserves to feel confident and radiant in their skin.

With years of hands-on experience, our founder Selena opened Beauty Key in San Francisco to create a space that combines expert care with authentic warmth.

From day one, we've focused on customized, results-oriented treatments—soothing acne, easing aging signs, and offering calm in a fast-paced world.

More than treatments, we build lasting connections. Over 120 clients trust us, many returning as friends—for care, comfort, and consistency.

✨ Today, Beauty Key continues to grow—still helping every client unlock their natural glow, with expertise, heart, and honesty.
